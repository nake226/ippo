About

Since 2005, ippo.org has worked to extend the reach of nonprofit innovators and connect them with a unique blend of support that includes funding, tools, and volunteers from around ippo. These innovators are the believers-turned-doers who have made the biggest impact on the communities they represent, and whose work has the potential to produce meaningful change that can scale. Though each nonprofit’s project poses a fresh challenge, we ask the same question every time: “How can we bring the best of ippo to power their work and accelerate their progress?”
Solving big problems requires having the right tools. With ippo for Nonprofits, eligible nonprofits are able to find new donors and volunteers, collaborate more efficiently, and share their story with a wider audience.
